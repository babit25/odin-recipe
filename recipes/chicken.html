Best griled Chicken Recipe

Grilled Chicken is a very popular recipe made with chicken, hung curd and spices like cumin powder and black pepper. It is a perfect chicken recipe for a quick meal and will be loved by everyone for its amazing flavours. This delicious and filling recipe is sure to woo your taste buds.

You can prepare this yummy dish for occasions like anniversaries and kitty parties. If you're planning to host a house party, then you must go for this chicken recipe as it is sure to impress your guests. Garlic paste adds a piquant aroma and flavour to this dish. So, wear your chef's hat and prepare this indulgent recipe to please your loved ones with your culinary skills.

Ingredients

  • 350 gm chicken
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  • salt as required
  • 1/2 cup hung curd
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1/4 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon powdered black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder

Steps

  1. Wash the chicken pieces and prick them with a fork.
  2. Now, in a large bowl, combine hung curd, ginger paste, garlic paste, red chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, black pepper, salt and garam masala.
  3. Stir all the ingredients together until well blended.
  4. Now add the chicken pieces and apply the masala mixture all over them.
  5. Cover and refrigerate for at least 8 hours or up to 24 hours.
  6. Once done, preheat the oven at 190 degree Celsius
  7. Remove the chicken pieces from the marinade and place them on a grilling tray.
  8. Grill them for 1 hour, flipping them after 30 minutes so that both sides can cook evenly.
  9. Check if the chicken has fully cooked by taking one piece out and cutting it with a knife.
  10. Serve hot with salad or a dip.
