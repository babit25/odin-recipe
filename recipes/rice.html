Curried Rice recipe

Best curried rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 cup rice
  • 2 cloves finely chopped garlic
  • 5 teaspoon curry powder
  • salt as required
  • 1 finely chopped onion
  • 2 1/2 tablespoon virgin olive oil
  • 4 ml water

Steps

  1. Prepare the saute mixture and add rice to it
  2. To start making this amazing recipe, heat olive oil in a heavy saucepan
  3. After the oil is hot enough, add chopped onions and garlic to it.
  4. Saute them on a medium heat until the onions turn transparent.
  5. Then add curry powder and rice to the saucepan and toss and cook for a minute.
  6. Add water and boil until the rice is cooked
  7. Then, add water and salt to the vessel and boil over a high heat without covering with a lid.
  8. Boil for 8 to 10 minutes or until the rice at the top start to appear dry.
  9. Reduce the heat to low, cover the container with a lid and cook the rice for another 10 minutes.
  10. Let the rice stand for a while and fluff it out
  11. Remove from heat and let the rice rest for 3-4 minutes.
  12. Toss and fluff the rice with a spoon and serve hot.
